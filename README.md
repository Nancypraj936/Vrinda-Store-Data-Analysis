# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis 2022

## Objective
Analyze Vrinda Store's 2022 sales data to identify trends and provide 
actionable business recommendations to increase revenue.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers, Dashboard)

## Dataset
- Vrinda Store order data for year 2022
- Fields include: Order ID, Category, Channel, State, Age, Gender, Order Status

## Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled null/blank values
- Standardized categorical columns (Gender, Status etc.)

## Dashboard Overview
The interactive dashboard includes:
- **Orders vs Sales** – Monthly trend (combo chart)
- **Sales: Men vs Women** – Pie chart (~65% women)
- **Order Status** – Pie chart (delivered, returned, cancelled, refunded)
- **Sales: Top 5 States** – Bar chart (Maharashtra highest at 2.93M)
- **Orders: Age vs Gender** – Grouped bar chart
- **Orders: Channels** – Pie chart (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra dominant)

### Slicers (Filters)
- Category, Month, Channel

## Key Insights
- Women are more likely to buy compared to men (~65%)
- Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh are top 3 states (~35%)
- Adult age group (30-49 yrs) is max contributing (~50%)
- Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra are max contributing channels (~80%)

## Final Conclusion
Target **women customers** aged **30-49 yrs** living in 
**Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh** by showing 
ads/offers/coupons on **Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra**.
